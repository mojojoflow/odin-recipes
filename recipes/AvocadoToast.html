Avocado Toast

The Millennials classic's

We can afford houses if we don't spend money on avocado toast, but it's too good!

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread, 2 slices for small avocado, 4 for large
  • Avocado
  • Nutrtional Yeast
  • Seasonig of choice:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Toast bread and cut avocado in half
  2. Scoop out avocado and apply to toast
  3. Add nooch(Nutrtional yeast) and choice Seasonings
  4. Enjoy these simple pleasures, who needs a house!
